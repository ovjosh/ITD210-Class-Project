Filter Replacement Program

7 Cities Appliance offers our Filter Replacement Program, where we will change your HVAC, refrigerator, home water filter, and any others you may have, on a regular interval as suggested by your manufacturer. There are several benefits to reguarly changing your filters. They include:


  • Extending the life of your Appliance
  • Helps to keep energy costs down
  • Maintains healthy air and water quality
  • Helps keep your heating/cooling and icemaker/water systems clean
